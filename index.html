About

Growing up in the suburbs of Chicago, I was lucky to have an awesome family that prioritized education and the value of learning. My mom worked as a kindergarten teacher for 30(!) years and instilled these values in each of her three boys.

When I went off to school at the University of Iowa, I followed in her footsteps with an interest in elementary education. Fast forward a few years, and I moved down to Raleigh, North Carolina with my first big job teaching 5th grade. I learned more in that first year of teaching than I ever could have in my studies, and all of the ups and downs made me the better for it.

I continued teaching 5th grade for a total of five years before I ultimately made the decision to continue my own learning and pursue my goals of becoming a software developer. I had to let go of my budding career, but the sacrifices were well worth it. At the Actualize Coding Bootcamp, I learned more in a few months than I ever dreamt was possible. My confidence as a developer grew and the reality of combining my two career interests soon became clear.
